Peru, Chiclayo

Official Flag Chiclayo

Chiclayo is located in northern Peru. It is known as the 'City of Friendship' and for its excellent dish,'Cabrito a la Norteña.' It has a beach called Pimentel, which is very popular with both foreigners and locals. Here lie the remains of a powerful 3rd-century ruler who governed Peru at that time.
